# LocalRoots Ambassador — what it is, what you make

**LocalRoots is a marketplace for neighbors to sell extra produce to neighbors.** No platform fees. No middleman taking a cut. The marketplace is live on Base mainnet today — real growers, real produce, real payments. The next thing we need is the people who introduce gardeners to it. That's you.

---

## What an ambassador actually does

You're a community organizer, not a marketer. You know — or quickly find — the gardeners in your neighborhood, your gardening club, your farmers market, your NextDoor group. You walk them through registering on LocalRoots (it's about 10–15 minutes per gardener). You help them list their first surplus harvest. You stay in touch as questions come up.

The platform handles transactions. You handle relationships.

## What you make

**25% of every sale** your recruited gardeners make for one full year. Paid in cash (Venmo, PayPal, or Zelle — your choice, set in your dashboard). This is real, live, and already running on mainnet.

You also earn **Roots Points** at the same rate, recorded on the Base blockchain. Roots Points are the proposed pre-launch reward — they convert to a future $ROOTS token at launch (allocation still being finalized; details on `localroots.love/about/tokenomics`).

If you also recruit other ambassadors, you keep **80%** of your direct gardeners' commissions and earn **20%** of the commissions of every ambassador *you* brought into the network. Compounding chain — the more people you bring in, the more your monthly earnings grow.

## Concrete numbers

- 10 gardeners averaging $100/month in sales = **$250/month cash** ($3,000/year), plus Roots Points
- Add 3 more ambassadors who each recruit 5 gardeners → **+$75/month** from the chain bonus
- 50 gardeners and a small downline = enough to be a meaningful side income

These are real numbers, not "potential." The 25% rate is the live program — it's how the math actually works right now.

## Why this matters

Most platforms launch a token first and hope something useful gets built. We're doing the opposite: build a marketplace neighbors actually use, prove the model works, then roll out broader rewards. Ambassadors are why the network grows region by region. Pre-launch ambassadors take real risk on something unproven — that's why the rates are generous, and why early ambassadors get visibility (your card is featured on `/ambassadors`).

## How to get started

1. Go to `localroots.love/ambassador/register`
2. Sign in with email or social (we use Privy — no crypto wallet needed)
3. Fill out your profile: name, bio, photo, payment preference (Venmo/PayPal/Zelle)
4. Get your personal referral link
5. Start with people you already know who garden — that's your highest-quality first wave

## Questions to ask yourself before signing up

- Do you know 5+ people who garden, or could find them quickly through your existing networks?
- Are you comfortable walking someone through a 10–15 minute online signup?
- Do you actually believe in local food and want to grow it in your area?

If yes, yes, and yes — you'll do well.
